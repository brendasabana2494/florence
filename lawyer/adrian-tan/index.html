NEXT PROFILE

Moses Musau is an associate with Florence Mwangangi and Co. Advocates, having joined the firm in 2017.

Moses prides himself in the expertise gathered in principal areas including Employment & Retirement Benefits, Civil & Commercial Litigation, Family & Property Law. He also has a knack for Constitutional and Human Rights litigation. In the short time he has been at the firm, he has had an excellent track record offering legal services, practising majorly in Litigation.

After earning his Bachelors Degree in Law at the Moi University, he went ahead and joined the Kenya School of law for his postgraduate diploma in Law. He was then Admitted as an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya in 2017. Moses has amassed invaluable knowledge and experience that has enabled him to handle legal issues in the most professional and effective way possible.
