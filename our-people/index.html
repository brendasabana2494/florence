About Us
Learned about the Law

Florence Mwangangi and Co. Advocates was founded in 1990 in Machakos town by the highly experienced and brilliant legal mind in Florence Mwangangi.
The firm then went ahead to open a head office in Nairobi’s Central Business District (CBD) in Shankardass House, Moi Avenue in 1991, and alongside the Machakos office, ran operations parallel until 2006, when operations in the Machakos office were discontinued. In 2007, for expansion purposes, the Main office was moved to Ajip House, Haile Selassie Avenue.

In 2019, the Main office was moved to a larger space in Hotel Connections Plaza, Mlolongo, along Mombasa Road where the firm currently handles its operations from. The firm holds an exceptional track record in the legal profession. It currently boasts an A-level service provision system that is fully digitalized to ensure its clients get unparalleled legal services.



In line with the remarkable firm ethos we at F M and Co. Advocates prides ourselves to, we have continuously rendered competent and satisfactory legal services to our clients. Our client base has in turn, grown over the years to remarkable numbers. We also boast a diversified number of practice areas, ranging from intellectual property law to constitutional litigation among other legal areas.

F M and Co. Advocates is also a socially responsible firm and we have been, and still are, actively engaged in community activities that aim at contributing to the betterment of the society. We therefore have a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) model installed.
